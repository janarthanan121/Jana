About

Welcome aboard Mac Airways, India's premier Domestic airline which provides you with one of the finest experiences across the skies. Our high standards of service and reliability, efficient operations and focus on innovation has enabled us become one of India's favourite domestic airline.

At Mac Airways, we take a holistic approach to corporate responsibility alongside our efforts to be India's best airline.


All of our efforts are focused around adding value: to our people, our communities and our Country. Our agenda is coordinated at the Mac Aviation Group level, to ensure that we have a cohesive programme of initiatives that covers all aspects of our business and operations.


All initiatives are conducted within Mac’s ‘Together’ framework.


Growing together
Support for the India Make, in India vision.


Greener together
Commitment to minimise our environmental impact.


Working together
The development and well-being of our diverse employees.


Giving together
Contributions to charities and humanitarian relief.
